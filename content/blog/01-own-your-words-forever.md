## But Why Does Permanence Matter?

"I don't need my tweets to last forever," you might think. Fair enough. But consider:

**For journalists**: Your investigation into corruption can't be silently removed when powerful people complain. The public record remains public.

**For researchers**: Your data, methodology, and findings are permanently citable. No more broken links in academic papers.

**For artists**: Your creative work is timestamped and attributed. Plagiarism becomes provable, not just claimable.

**For historians**: Future generations can access primary sources, not just what platforms decided to preserve.

**For you**: The blog post you write today can still be read by your grandchildren. Your digital legacy is actually... a legacy.
